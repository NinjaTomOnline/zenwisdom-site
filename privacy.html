Privacy Policy

Privacy for Zen Wisdom

Zen Wisdom is designed as a local-first reflection app in its current build. Most of the information you create inside the app stays on your device unless you choose to contact support directly.

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Stored on device

Journal entries, favorites, meditation progress, and appearance settings

The current build stores your reflection history and app preferences locally on your device using the app’s own data store.

Shared only if you choose to

Support email

If you email support, your message and any attachments are handled by your email provider and the Zen Wisdom support inbox so the team can respond.

Not in the current build

No account system, cloud sync, or third-party ad tracking

The repo snapshot does not include user accounts, advertising SDKs, or remote analytics collection.

Information Zen Wisdom stores locally

  • Journal prompts, moods, and reflection text that you save
  • Saved favorite quotes
  • Meditation playback progress and completion state
  • Theme, background, and font preferences
  • Local premium entitlement state cached after App Store purchase or restore checks

Information Zen Wisdom does not currently collect remotely

  • No account profile or sign-in details
  • No remote journal backup in this repo snapshot
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No third-party analytics SDK events sent from the current build

Support communication

If you contact support at UrbanZenMaster@gmail.com, the team uses your message only to respond to your request, troubleshoot the issue you reported, or follow up on product questions.

Purchases

Premium purchases and restores are handled through Apple’s StoreKit and App Store systems. Zen Wisdom keeps local entitlement state so the app can show premium access after Apple verifies a purchase or restore.

Your choices

  • You can delete individual journal entries from the app.
  • You can remove saved favorites inside the app.
  • You can contact support if you need help understanding the current release behavior.

Questions about privacy

For privacy-related questions, email UrbanZenMaster@gmail.com and include “Privacy” in the subject line.
